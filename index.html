Hi, thanks for dropping by my traveler blog !

My name is Ellen. I am a photographer and also a traveler. I have been pursuing portraits since I was a teenager until now. For me a photo can save a million memories. I have explored many beautiful places. Starting from forests, historic sites, oceans, famous restaurants, traditional markets, mountains, etc. In this world there are many beautiful places that can be used as ports for us to unwind. Through this lens I can capture many moments in it that can never be repeated. Beautiful views that I will never be able to get anywhere else. I love this job. Even though it is quite difficult to be able to get the right moment to produce natural and beautiful photo shots, by working like this I feel that my life is freer and I really enjoy it. When it works according to what you like it is a very extraordinary thing. It's part of our life, so we better enjoy every process!
